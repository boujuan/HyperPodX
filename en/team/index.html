The Team

We are Students from the University of Oldenburg and the University of Applied Sciences Emden-Leer. Nationalities from all over the world: China, India, Nicaragua, France, Costa Rica, Germany, Nepal, Spain, Austria, and England. Team HyperPodX is a diverse group seeking to develop the technology of the future. Our Multidisciplinary and international environment allows this project to be an experience of growth for everybody and a challenge that enables our team to compete with top universities from all over the word.

Although the team consists of experienced people who are familiar with teamwork, this Challenge ahead of us will bring us a lot closer together and grow strong bonds for the future. Not only bonds within the team, but also bonds with industry and other researchers. This way the project also gives opportunities for further Job applications and a lot of practical knowledge to the team. The knowledge gained exceeds the normal content of lectures by far.
