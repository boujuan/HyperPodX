The Team

Our 50-member team, (accompanied by professors Prof. Dr. Walter Neu and Prof. Dr. Thomas Schüning from the Technical Department of the University of Applied Sciences Emden/Leer), is the only North German team to be admitted to the "Hyperloop Pod Competition II" finals.

All of our highly motivated team members are fully committed to the project. A project so close to real-life practice is rare, and it is a unique opportunity to gain practical experience during our studies. In particular, the application of an entrepreneurial mindset and general management competencies are developed by working in this team.
