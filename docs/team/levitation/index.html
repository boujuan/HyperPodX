Levitation

The seven-person levitation system team is responsible for ensuring a stable flight of the pod. With our passive magnetic levitation system, it is possible for us to levitate without contact to the tracks, and thus, overcome the braking force due to mechanical friction. It is important that the efficiency is as high as possible, which can be increased both by the selection of the magnets and their arrangement, as well as by optimizing the overall system design.
